## Idea 1: Cyberpunk Dojo

**Design Movement:** Cyberpunk meets feudal Japan — neon-lit dojo aesthetic

**Core Principles:**
- Dark near-black backgrounds with neon lime green as the singular accent
- Comic-book illustrated characters (space ninjas) as the primary visual language
- Heavy, condensed display typography that commands attention
- Wavy organic dividers between sections (like Megalodon) but with nebula/cosmic energy feel

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: #050a00 (near-black with a hint of green)
- Accent: #b8ff00 (electric lime — matches Kairo's existing brand exactly)
- Secondary: #1a0a2e (deep space purple for card backgrounds)
- Text: Pure white on dark, lime on dark for highlights

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero with illustration on the right, copy on the left
- Alternating left/right content sections
- 4-column service card grid with illustrated backgrounds
- Wavy SVG dividers between major sections

**Signature Elements:**
- Space ninja illustrations as the core visual motif
- Neon green glow effects on key elements
- Particle/star field in dark sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll with parallax on illustrations
- Hover states reveal neon green glow on cards
- CTA buttons pulse with subtle green glow animation

**Animation:**
- Entrance animations: fade-up with 30-80ms stagger
- Floating particles in hero background
- Subtle parallax on hero illustration
- Card hover: lift + neon border glow

**Typography System:**
- Display: Bebas Neue (condensed, bold, commanding)
- Body: DM Sans (clean, modern, readable)
- Accent labels: Space Mono (monospace for tech feel)
